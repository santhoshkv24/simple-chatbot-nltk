SRM University AP is a private university located in Neerukonda, Amaravati, Andhra Pradesh. It is known for its focus on research, innovation, and global collaborations. The university offers undergraduate and postgraduate courses in Engineering, Management, Sciences, and Humanities. It is recognized by the University Grants Commission (UGC) and has partnerships with top institutions like MIT and UC Berkeley.

The campus provides modern facilities, including well-equipped laboratories, a central library with digital and physical resources, advanced research centers, and state-of-the-art classrooms. The university has a vibrant student community, with various clubs and societies that foster extracurricular and co-curricular activities.

SRM AP offers hostel facilities for both boys and girls, with comfortable accommodations and dining services. The campus also has recreational facilities such as sports complexes, gymnasiums, and cultural centers to ensure a well-rounded student experience.

The university has an excellent placement record, with top recruiters like Amazon, Microsoft, and Google offering job opportunities to students. The placement cell provides career guidance, training, and internship opportunities to enhance employability.

Admissions to SRM AP are conducted through SRMJEEE for engineering programs, while other courses have direct admission or entrance-based selection processes. The university offers scholarships based on merit, sports achievements, and financial background to support students in their academic journey.

SRM AP provides a well-connected transportation facility for students and faculty from nearby cities. The university's commitment to academic excellence, research innovation, and student development makes it one of the leading emerging universities in India.
